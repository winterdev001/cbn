BIEN NAÎTRE CLINIC PROFILE

In 2000, Dr Nkundabagenzi Secyugu Jean Claude, was working with a public organization Hospital. Where he met many high risk pregnancy and saw that they need a special care and specialized treatment. And Decide to start a privet Clinique. This was our beginning!
Since as early as 2006, the statutes of the BIEN NAÎTRE clinic has been placing emphasis on the quality of pregnant women especially high risk cases. This tradition remains a priority for us, but going forward it includes as well the smooth operation of all services:

  • Care is regularly adapted to the evolving healthcare needs of the population,
  • Cutting-edge medical practices are adopted as soon as they are proven reliable,
  • Technical support centers are reconfigured in keeping with innovations,
  • The development of funding methods and health policy repercussions are taken into account.
Patients benefit on a regular basis from initiatives and innovations generated by the dynamism and professionalism of the members of our clinic:
  • Special care and treatment of high risk pregnancies,
  • gynecological laparoscopy surgery,
  • prenatal and postnatal diagnosis
  • More Treatment and of very high risk pregnancies

Leaders, in region in obstetric & gynecologist both in delivery, surgery and hospitalization and much more psychosocial life of the mother ant the new born.
He saw that the region was in a great need of a specialized privet Clinique that would benefit who lived and worked at the hospital in a spirit of devotion and self-denial, as instructed by the order of Bien Naître Clinic.
The Clinic has opened the door in 2006. In 2007 we have been officially certified by the Ministry of Health to operate as a specialized Clinique. From then now we are in this for 13years of experiences.
We have built trust and customer for life culture that has brought many women to our clinic and we are still receiving much more new client and cases which has increased both our career experience and customer experience.
